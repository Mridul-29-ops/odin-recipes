PIZZA

Description

Pizza is a beloved Italian dish featuring a round, flat crust topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and a variety of delicious toppings like pepperoni, mushrooms, or veggies. It's baked to perfection and served hot.

Whether thin and crispy or thick and chewy, pizza is perfect for any meal and loved worldwide.

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough base
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup sliced pepperoni
  • 1/4 cup sliced bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup sliced mushrooms
  • Dried oregano and chili flakes (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 220°C (425°F).
  2. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the pizza base.
  3. Sprinkle cheese and arrange toppings of your choice.
  4. Bake in the oven for 12–15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
  5. Remove, slice, and serve hot with a sprinkle of oregano or chili flakes if desired.
