PASTA

Description

Pasta is a classic Italian staple made from wheat flour and water, formed into various shapes and typically boiled. It pairs wonderfully with sauces like marinara, Alfredo, or pesto, and can be customized with vegetables, meats, or cheese.

Whether served as a main course or a side, pasta is a comforting, hearty, and endlessly versatile dish loved all around the world.

Ingredients

  • 200g (7 oz) dried pasta (spaghetti, penne, or your choice)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook according to package instructions until al dente.
  2. Meanwhile, heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Add minced garlic and sauté for about 1 minute until fragrant.
  3. Add tomato sauce and dried basil to the pan. Simmer for 5–7 minutes. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Drain the pasta and add it to the sauce. Toss to combine and heat for another minute.
  5. Serve hot, topped with grated Parmesan cheese if desired.
