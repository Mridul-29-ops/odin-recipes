TACOS

Description

Tacos are a traditional Mexican dish made with small, folded tortillas filled with flavorful ingredients like seasoned meats, beans, vegetables, and cheese. They're often topped with salsa, sour cream, or fresh cilantro.

Easy to customize and quick to prepare, tacos are a crowd-pleasing favorite for any meal or gathering.

Ingredients

  • 6 small taco tortillas (corn or flour)
  • 1/2 lb (225g) ground beef or chicken
  • 1 tablespoon taco seasoning
  • 1/2 cup chopped lettuce
  • 1/2 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • Sour cream and salsa (optional)

Steps

  1. In a pan over medium heat, cook the ground beef or chicken until browned. Drain excess fat if needed.
  2. Add taco seasoning and a splash of water. Stir and simmer for 3â€“5 minutes until well coated.
  3. Warm tortillas in a skillet or microwave until soft.
  4. Fill each tortilla with the cooked meat, lettuce, tomatoes, and cheese.
  5. Top with sour cream, salsa, or your favorite toppings. Serve immediately.
